# Design Brief

## Product

Professional photo gallery platform for photographers.

## Visual Direction

- Elegant
- Minimal
- Premium
- Editorial
- Clean
- High-end photography portfolio feel

## Audience

Photographers, studios, and their clients.

## UI Goals

- Photos must be the main focus
- Interface should feel luxurious but simple
- Avoid clutter
- Mobile-first
- Fast gallery browsing
- Clear selection experience

## Style

- Neutral colors
- Lots of whitespace
- Large image previews
- Subtle shadows
- Smooth interactions
- Elegant typography

## Avoid

- Generic SaaS dashboard look
- Loud gradients
- Too many colors
- Overdesigned cards
- Cluttered navigation
